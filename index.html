Q28 · Box A gives 36 apples to Box B · 4 units more
How many apples did the 4 extra units hold?
Box A gave away 36 apples. Box B received 36 apples. Remember — every time 1 apple moves, the change is 2. Box A lost 36 and Box B gained 36 — so altogether that is 36 + 36 = 72. After all 36 apples moved, both boxes were exactly equal — all 4 extra units were completely gone. So 4 units is 72 apples.
Q28 · Internal transfer concept · apples
What is special about an internal transfer question?
In an internal transfer question, every time something moves from one side to the other — whoever gives has 1 fewer, and whoever gets has 1 more. That is a change of 2 in total. Let's see why, using the example below.
